Malala Yousafzai

A girl who lives for rights.

Here's a time line of Malala Yousafzai :

  • 1997 - Taliban rules Pakistan
    July 12, 1997 : Malala was born.
  • 2008 - Sep 1 , 2008 : Malala has her first speech.
    October 7, 2008 : Taliban had blown up two schools
    Malala wins the International Children's Peace Prize.
    Malala gets a death threat.
  • 2012 - October 9, 2012 : Malala gets shot.
  • 2013 - July 12, 2013 : Malala Won the United Nations Human Rights Prize
  • 2014 - October 10, 2014 : Malala received the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 2014 - October 21, 2014 : Malala wins the Liberty Medal.
  • 2015 - July 12, 2015 : She opened a Girls' school.
  • 1944 - July 20, 2017 : Pakistan's president launched a ten million Education fund.

"Yousafzai, Malala, 1997- author. I Am Malala : the Girl Who Stood up for Education and Was Shot by the Taliban. New York, NY :Little, Brown, & Company, 2013."
